## Design Tokens

**Typography**:
- **Display Font**: Bold, modern (e.g., Poppins Bold, Outfit Bold) — for headlines
- **Body Font**: Clean, readable (e.g., Inter, Roboto) — for body text and descriptions

**Spacing**:
- Generous whitespace between sections (80-120px)
- Consistent padding within cards and sections

**Colors**:
- Primary: Cyan/Blue (from Kosi logo)
- Secondary: Deep Navy (#1E2D35)
- Accent: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for backgrounds
- Text: Dark Gray (#333333) on light backgrounds

**Shadows & Depth**:
- Subtle shadows on cards (minimal, professional)
- No heavy gradients — keep it clean

**Responsive Design**:
- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints: 640px (tablet), 1024px (desktop)
